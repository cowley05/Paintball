Kids special. Play from the age of 8.

At Combat Paintball we like to cater for everyone, that's why we have designed a package specifically to accommodate the younger generation from 8+. This incorporates a low impact paintball gun making it ideal for younger children and those who are uncertain about traditional paintball. Those new to the experience will start by practising on a target range. Afterwards it's off we go to the village and the fortress for 5 games of paintball. This is a fantastic introduction to paintball that the kids will talk about for weeks. The kids special is a flat rate for the whole group and MUST be paid in full at least one week in advance to reserve a game. There is no penalty for falling short of the 10 players since this is a flat rate package.
