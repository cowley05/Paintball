What do we do?

Combat Paintball is a fantastic day out for all occasions especially for birthday parties, stag/hen do's and team building days. At Combat Paintball we specialise in catering for all groups of different types whether it be children (13+), adrenaline junkies or Rambo imitators.

Where are we?

We are situated in Lathom, 5 minutes from Skelmersdale and Ormskirk. Just a short distance from M58 which is easily accessible from Liverpool, Manchester, St Helens, Warrington, Bolton and Blackpool. See below for exact details and how to find us.

Fun and excitement. No matter what you enjoy!

Our games are all designed to allow teams to work together and communicate whilst completing the objective of the game. Games can be played as fast or slow paced as you make it, whether you love to run around senseless or camp off like a sniper all is possible at Combat Paintball. We have many games involving flags, balls, bells and dummies all surrounded by woodland combined with trucks, towers and forts.
